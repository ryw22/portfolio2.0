ABOUT

Rachel is a second year undergraduate student at Georgia Tech studying Computational Media with a concentration in Intelligence and Interaction Design. She is an artist who enjoys creating traditional and digital art. Some artists she looks up to are Takashi Murakami and Kerby Rosanes. Her favorite artwork is Water Lilies by Monet. She began drawing at a young age and hopes to continue it in her career, which led her be interested in pursuing a future in UI/UX design. In her free time, she enjoys doodling on Procreate and playing around with Adobe Photoshop. In the works tab, you are able to see her previous and most recent works.
