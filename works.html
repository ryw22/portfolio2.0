American Bison

    • A State of Dichotomy

  • Medium: Prismacolor Pencil
  • 19.5 inch x 25.5 inch Mint Color paper
  • Process: The artwork represents my passion for dance. The worn-out ballet shoes illustrate the hard work a dancer puts in. The contrast between the soft fabric of the ballet shoe and the hard metal horseshoe signifies the hard determination to polish a dance choreography. The ladybug symbolizes good luck which is what my friends and I would say to each other before a performance.
